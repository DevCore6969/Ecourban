Energy Saving Tips

Switch to LED

Replace traditional bulbs with energy-efficient LED bulbs to save up to 80% on lighting costs.

Optimize Thermostat

Set your thermostat to 20°C in winter and 25°C in summer for optimal comfort and energy savings.

Use Natural Ventilation

Open windows during cooler hours to reduce AC usage and improve indoor air quality.

Unplug Unused Devices

Many devices consume power even when turned off. Unplug them when not in use.
